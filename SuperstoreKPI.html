Superstore KPI Management

In this project, a Tableau dashboard is crafted to illustrate the sales and profits of a superstore across various states.
The analysis reveals a seasonality pattern in the KPIs during the fall and winter months. Furthermore, an anomalous spike detected in the time-series sales data raises concerns, highlighting potential issues within a specific state's store.
